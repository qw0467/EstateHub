# EstateHub — YouTube Video Script
### Target Duration: 6 minutes exactly

---

## INTRO [0:00 – 0:30]

*(Speaker 1 on camera)*

Qatar is one of the fastest-growing real estate markets in the world. Thousands of people move here every year — and every single one of them needs to find a place to live. But the process of finding, booking, and managing property viewings in Qatar is still stuck in the past. You browse a website, you call an agent, you send a WhatsApp message, and then you wait.

**>>> PRESS: Switch to screen recording — show the EstateHub home page loading <<<**

We built EstateHub to change that.

---

## THE PROBLEM [0:30 – 1:15]

*(Speaker 2 on camera)*

Right now, if you want to find a property in Qatar, you go to a site like Property Finder or Bayut. You scroll through listings, you find one you like, and then — you hit a wall. There's no way to book a viewing. There's no way to know if a time slot is available. There's no way to list your own property if you're not a licensed agent. And if you're a seller, you have no idea who's interested in your property or when they want to visit.

**>>> PRESS: Show a split screen — Property Finder on the left, EstateHub on the right <<<**

Every platform on the market is a one-way street. Buyers browse. Sellers list through agents. Nothing connects them directly. EstateHub closes that gap.

---

## OUR SOLUTION [1:15 – 2:00]

*(Speaker 1 on camera)*

EstateHub is a full real estate platform built specifically for Qatar. Any user can browse properties, any registered user can list their own property, and buyers can book viewings directly on the platform — no phone calls, no WhatsApp, no waiting.

**>>> PRESS: Show the Properties page — scroll slowly through the listings grid <<<**

We built a real-time booking system where you pick a date, and you see a grid of every available time slot — colour coded. White means available. Grey means it's already passed. Red means someone else already booked it. You pick your slot, confirm, and you're done.

**>>> PRESS: Navigate to a property and click Book a Viewing — show the calendar and time slot grid <<<**

---

## HOW IT'S BETTER [2:00 – 2:45]

*(Speaker 2 on camera)*

No other property platform in Qatar gives private individuals the ability to list their own property without going through an agent. On EstateHub, any registered user can create a listing in under two minutes.

**>>> PRESS: Go to Profile → My Listings → click New Listing — show the listing form briefly <<<**

And here's something no competitor offers at all — sellers can see exactly who has booked viewings on their properties. Name, email, date, time. All in one place. You can even email the buyer directly from the platform with one click.

**>>> PRESS: Go to Profile → Enquiries tab — show an incoming booking card with the Email Buyer button <<<**

Property Finder? Bayut? None of them have this. Once you post a listing, you're in the dark.

---

## MEMBERSHIP AND PREMIUM FEATURES [2:45 – 3:45]

*(Speaker 1 on camera)*

EstateHub also has a tiered membership system. Free users get full access to standard listings and the booking system. But for serious buyers and investors, we offer Monthly and Yearly Premium plans.

**>>> PRESS: Navigate to the Membership page — show the three pricing cards <<<**

Premium members get exclusive access to luxury properties that never appear on public listings. They get early access to new listings 48 hours before everyone else. And they get access to our Investment Analysis tool, which calculates estimated rental yield, monthly income, and break-even period for any property on the platform.

**>>> PRESS: Go to Members → Investment Analysis tab — select a property and show the yield calculation and chart <<<**

Yearly Premium members also get a personal concierge service — they can book a one-to-one consultation with an agent to discuss any property, and they get invited to exclusive networking events.

**>>> PRESS: Click on the Concierge Service tab — show the booking form <<<**

---

## DEMO — END-TO-END FLOW [3:45 – 5:15]

*(Speaker 2 on camera — narrate over screen recording)*

Let me show you the full experience from start to finish.

**>>> PRESS: Start from the home page <<<**

A new user lands on EstateHub. They can immediately browse properties without creating an account.

**>>> PRESS: Click Browse Properties — scroll the grid for 5 seconds <<<**

They find a property they like, click on it, and read the full details — photos, square footage, features, price.

**>>> PRESS: Click on any property card — show the detail page <<<**

They decide they want to see it. They sign up — takes about 30 seconds — and they're in.

**>>> PRESS: Navigate to /auth — show the sign-up form and T&C scroll <<<**

Now they click Book a Viewing. They pick a date on the calendar. The time slot grid appears. They can immediately see which slots are free and which are taken.

**>>> PRESS: Go through the full booking flow — pick a date, select a white time slot, click Confirm <<<**

The booking is confirmed instantly and saved to their profile.

**>>> PRESS: Navigate to Profile → Appointments tab — show the confirmed booking <<<**

Now let's switch to the seller's perspective. They log in to their account and check their Enquiries tab.

**>>> PRESS: Switch to the seller account — go to Profile → Enquiries <<<**

The booking just made by our buyer is right there — with their email address and the exact time they want to visit.

**>>> PRESS: Hover over the Email Buyer button <<<**

One click and the seller is talking to a qualified buyer. No agents, no middlemen, no delay.

---

## CLOSING [5:15 – 6:00]

*(Speaker 1 on camera)*

EstateHub is built with React, TypeScript, and Supabase — a modern, scalable tech stack that gives us real-time data, secure user authentication, and a fast, responsive experience on any device.

**>>> PRESS: Show the app on a mobile phone screen (or use Chrome DevTools mobile view) <<<**

The problem we set out to solve was real. Buyers and sellers in Qatar deserve a platform that actually connects them — not one that stops at a phone number and a prayer. EstateHub does that.

**>>> PRESS: Return to the EstateHub home page — let it sit for 3 seconds <<<**

We're EstateHub. Thank you.

**>>> PRESS: Fade to black — show team name and university on screen <<<**
